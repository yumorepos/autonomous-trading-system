## 3. Canary Trading Rules (Trades 1–20)

### Position Limits
| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Max position size | $12 | 12% of capital |
| Max concurrent positions | 1 | One experiment at a time |
| Max new trades per day | 2 | Prevent chasing |
| Min time between trades | 4 hours | Cooling period |
| Max daily loss | $3 | 3% of capital |
| Max total drawdown | $15 | 15% of capital → HALT |

### Entry Rules
A signal may execute if ALL of the following are true:
1. Multi-factor composite score ≥ 6.0
2. At least 2/3 signal confirmations (funding + momentum + volume)
3. Funding direction gate passes (Gate #11)
4. No existing open position
5. Daily loss < $3
6. Total drawdown < $15
7. Circuit breaker not tripped (< 3 consecutive losses)
8. Not within 4 hours of last trade

### Skip Rules (signal passes but we DON'T trade)
- Asset is in top-3 by 24h volume decline (liquidity drying up)
- Funding rate changed direction in last 2 hours (unstable)
- More than 5 signals pass simultaneously (regime anomaly, not asset-specific)
- It's the 3rd trade attempt today

### HALT Rules (pause entire system)
- 3 consecutive losses → pause 24 hours
- $15 total drawdown from peak → pause indefinitely, manual review
- Any unplanned execution (trade without pre-decision packet) → immediate halt

## 8. Guardrails

1. **No parameter tweaking from one trade.** Minimum 3 occurrences of same failure pattern before changing anything.
2. **No claiming repo is clean without remote verification.** Always fresh clone + scan.
3. **No public posting of wallet-linked evidence.** Ever.
4. **No trade-count chasing.** If signals don't pass, we don't trade. Empty weeks are acceptable.
5. **No scaling without ALL gates passing.** No exceptions, no "close enough."
6. **No manual overrides.** If the system says skip, we skip.
7. **Every trade must have a pre-decision packet.** No packet = no trade.
8. **Every closed trade must have a post-evidence packet.** No evidence = doesn't count toward scaling.
